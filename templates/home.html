About Us

FOR A CAUSE provides a charity fundraising ecosystem to the users of Amazon voice-service enabled devices and charities wanting to raise donations. We allow users to discover and donate to the causes that speak to them (pun intended!).

Payments can be set up via AmazonPay.

This site is designed for the charities to register with us, so their information can be used on our Alexa voice skill.
